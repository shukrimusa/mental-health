Sadness

Sadness is a natural and temporary emotional state characterized by feelings of unhappiness, sorrow, or grief. It often arises in response to various life events, such as loss, disappointment, or change. Physically, sadness may be accompanied by a heavy heart, tears, or a sense of lethargy. Unlike clinical depression, which is a persistent mental health condition, sadness is a normal part of the human experience. It can serve as a valuable emotional signal, prompting reflection and coping mechanisms. While temporary, prolonged or intense sadness may warrant attention, acknowledging and expressing sadness within a supportive environment is a healthy aspect of emotional well-being.
